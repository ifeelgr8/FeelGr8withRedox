What Are Redox Signaling Molecules?

Your body is made up of cells — and inside those cells are tiny messengers called redox signaling molecules. They help your cells communicate, recognize damage, and respond. Think of them like your body’s internal text messages, telling your system when something’s wrong and activating the healing process.

As we age or go through stress, we produce fewer of them. That’s where redox technology comes in — it helps restore this natural communication so your body can repair, recover, and function more efficiently.
